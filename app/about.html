About Us


What is "Mursheed" ?

Are not you tired of searching for travel agencies, negotiating, comparing, asking for discounts? As a result, you just face with unpleasant surprises and return to your country with bad impressions about your dream vacation.

"Mursheed " helps you to avoid wasting your time and the disappointment of such an experience which will be anyway below your expectations at the end of your trip.

Murshed is a program which helps tourists get access to more opportunities with minimum or no risks. Our start up in Azerbaijan, Georgia and Turkey, but we are going to serve you worldwide.

Here, we are providing you with premium services in less time and for less price, without any extra fees of third parts.

So just pack your bag and on the way to the airport you will hire your driver with the requested language skills and the desired car. Book your flat or villa, make your reservation for each day at different restaurants, even book a yacht for dinner in the open sea and make surprise for your friends...

All drivers are professional and applaudable. They are chosen by us. Your online payment, transactions, and safety are under our responsibility. Ratings and feedback will help you to choose the right person and place.

Besides all of these, you can find enough information and history about the places that you are going to visit.

Our 24/7 call center will help you to get the best solutions for any issues in just a few seconds.
